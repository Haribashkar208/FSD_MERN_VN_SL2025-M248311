Key Areas of Advancement:

  • Generative & Multimodal AI: Models like Google's Gemini 3 and OpenAI's GPT-5 process various inputs (text, images, video, audio) to create content and provide deep insights, powering tools like Sora (video generation) and advanced design AI.
  • Agentic AI & Assistants: AI agents are evolving to perform complex, multi-step tasks autonomously, while personalized assistants (like Perplexity's shopping assistant) offer tailored help, notes this YouTube video and this Appinventiv article.
  • Edge AI: Processing data locally on devices (smartphones, vehicles) for real-time action, better privacy, and lower network demands, using specialized low-power chips.
  • AI in Science & Healthcare: Accelerating drug discovery (AI-designed drugs, protein modeling with AlphaFold), personalized medicine, and simulating complex biomolecular dynamics.
  • Cybersecurity: AI-driven scam shields (like Google's Call Screen) detect deepfakes and fraud in real-time, protecting users.

AI is a field of computer science focused on creating smart machines that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Artificial neural networks, a core component of AI, are inspired by the structure and function of the human brain.
